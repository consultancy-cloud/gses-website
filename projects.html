Selected Work

Owners Engineer

NSW Health Solar & Storage Program

NSW Health / Health Infrastructure NSW

GSES delivered Owners Engineering, independent technical due diligence, and treasury-grade financial modelling to enable funding through the NSW Treasury Loan Fund. The John Hunter Hospital rooftop solar installation was the largest on any hospital globally at commissioning. The broader program spans 15+ sites including Gosford Hospital (1.6 MWp preliminary design) and feasibility assessments for 30+ NSW hospital PV systems.

Inspection

QA compliance inspections across 46 sites and 48 PV systems statewide — four coordinated inspection trips, 4 CEC-accredited multi-state inspectors, and data uploaded to Diagno. A separate engagement also covered 16-battery-system inspections.
