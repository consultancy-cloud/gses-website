The Team

Our Directors

Christopher Martell
Managing Director

Chris is a Chartered Professional Engineer (CPEng, IntPE) with a Masters in Photovoltaic Engineering from UNSW and over 15 years in the solar and battery industry. He joined GSES in 2013 and has led the organisation as Managing Director since 2022, overseeing consultancy, inspection, and engineering programs across government and commercial clients nationwide.

Before GSES, Chris was a PV Engineer at Solgen Energy, where his team won the Clean Energy Council Award for Best Grid-Connected PV System over 100kW in Australia. He has served as Operating Agent for IEA PVPS Task 18 and on the Steering Committee of the Australian Energy Storage Alliance. He is also co-founder and Director of Diagno Energy, a solar monitoring and analytics platform, and a Non-Executive Director of Mindaro.
